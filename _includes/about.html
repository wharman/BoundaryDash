About Boundary Dash


Boundary Dash is project founded by Wendy Harman to help you redefine the edges of your organization.

Connect with the right volunteers, donors, partners, members, and advocates at the right time.

Boundary Dash uses human-centered design, digital strategy, and data to optimize your relationships.

Wendy is passionate about helping your nonprofit, foundation, or civic organization understand how to scale community engagement for maximum impact.
